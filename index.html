Transformation Case Studies

Real results for educational institutions

Workflow Visibility Transformation

Challenge: A university had critical workflow data spread across 4 separate systems (SQL Server, Oracle), with departments unable to track turnaround times or bottlenecks.

Solution: We implemented an Azure Data Factory ETL pipeline to aggregate and transform disparate data sources into a unified Azure SQL database, powering Tableau dashboards for real-time workflow monitoring.

Outcome: 33% reduction in processing time by identifying and eliminating bottlenecks, with complete transparency across three previously siloed departments.

Student Success Early Warning System

Challenge: A community college struggled to identify at-risk students early enough for effective intervention.

Solution: Integrated LMS, SIS, and attendance data through a modern data stack, creating AI-powered predictive models to flag students needing support.

Outcome: Improved retention by 18% through earlier interventions and reduced advisor workload by 25% through automated prioritization.
